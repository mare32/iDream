Welcome!

Dreams are a phenomena which we are all familiar with. Lucid dreams however, are a bit more of a taboo subject regarding an average person since it requires an investment of time and effort for something that doesn't even happen in our reality, and it seems silly for the 95% of the people.. but is it really?

Introduction to Lucid Dreaming

Being "Lucid" means being aware of something, thus lucid dreaming means being fully conscious and aware that you are in a dream.

Methods for lucid dreaming

Method is the only way you can induce lucidity. There are 2 methods: WILD(Wake Induced Lucid Dream)-Falling asleep consciously, and DILD(Dream Induced Lucid Dream)-Realising you are dreaming while being in a dream.

Lucid dreaming techniques

There are many techniques so i will just mention several:
-Reality checks
-Dream journaling
-Mnemonicly Induced Lucid Dream
-Wake Back To Bed
-Dream Chaining

Pros and Cons of lucid dreaming

Pros:are having infinite amounts of fun, unlimited creativity and also exploring your mind and getting rid of bad thoughts.
Cons:Confusing reality with dreams, happens to think you are inside a dream but you are actualy not.
